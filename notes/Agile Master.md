###### trusted source for PMP Certificate  
فيه اصلا prerequisites محتاجينها في البداية قبل ما نخش و نشوف المحتوي و ناخد الشهادة. 
The main prerequisites for taking the PMP exam are:

A four-year university degree (or the global equivalent)
3 years (36 months) of non-overlapping professional project management experience
4,500 hours leading and directing projects
35 contact hours of training in project management
OR
A high school diploma, associate degree, or global equivalent
5 years (60 months) of non-overlapping professional project management experience
7,500 hours leading and directing projects
35 contact hours of training in project management
